Privacy and Security Policy

At Diplomax, we take your privacy and security seriously. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

  • Personal details like name, email address, and contact number during registration or form submissions.
  • Technical data such as browser type, IP address, and operating system.
  • Usage data including pages visited and interaction behavior.

How We Use Your Information

  • To improve website functionality and user experience.
  • To provide personalized services and notifications.
  • To contact you for feedback, updates, and important notices.

Security Measures

We implement the following security practices to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption on all pages.
  • Secure data storage and limited access protocols.
  • Regular software updates and vulnerability assessments.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance user experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though it may affect some features.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties unless required by law or with your explicit consent.
