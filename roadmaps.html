Diploma Engineering Career Roadmaps
(All Branches)

Computer Science Engineering (CSE)

  • Semester 1-3: Focus on C, C++, Data Structures
  • Semester 4-5: Learn Web Dev (HTML, CSS, JS), Python, Java
  • Semester 6: Internship + Major Project (Choose trending domain)
  • After Diploma:
    • D2D BTech CSE or Join Job as Junior Dev
    • Learn DSA, Git, GitHub
    • Choose Domain: Web Dev, App Dev, AI, Cyber Security

Electrical Engineering (EE)

  • Semester 1-3: Basic Electricals, Circuit Theory, Machines
  • Semester 4-5: PLC, Microcontrollers, MATLAB, AutoCAD Electrical
  • Semester 6: Internship + Project (Smart Grid, Automation)
  • After Diploma:
    • D2D BTech EE or Join Core Company
    • Learn Embedded Systems, Power Electronics, IoT
    • Get Certified (SCADA, Solar Tech, Electric Vehicle Tech)

Mechanical Engineering (ME)

  • Semester 1-3: Engineering Drawing, Thermodynamics, SOM
  • Semester 4-5: CAD, CAM, CNC, SolidWorks
  • Semester 6: Industrial Internship + Project
  • After Diploma:
    • D2D BTech ME or Join Industry as Technician
    • Get certified in Design/Manufacturing Tools
    • Explore domains: Automobile, Robotics, Aerospace

Civil Engineering

  • Semester 1-3: Basics of Surveying, Materials, Structures
  • Semester 4-5: AutoCAD, Revit, STAAD Pro, Estimation
  • Semester 6: Internship on-site + Real project
  • After Diploma:
    • D2D BTech Civil or Join Construction/Design Firms
    • Learn Quantity Surveying, GIS, Project Mgmt
    • Govt Exams: JE, SSC, RRB, PWD

Electronics & Communication (ECE)

  • Semester 1-3: Digital Electronics, Analog Circuits, Signals
  • Semester 4-5: VLSI, Embedded, IoT, PCB Design
  • Semester 6: Project in Communication/Robotics
  • After Diploma:
    • D2D BTech ECE or Join Electronics/IT Firm
    • Courses: MATLAB, Arduino, IoT Dev
    • Domains: Telecom, IoT, Embedded Systems

Information Technology (IT)

  • Semester 1-3: Programming Fundamentals, DBMS, OS
  • Semester 4-5: Learn Full Stack, Android, Cyber Security
  • Semester 6: Portfolio Project + Internship
  • After Diploma:
    • D2D BTech IT or Freelance/Remote Jobs
    • Specialize in DevOps, Cloud, Security, AI
    • Build Resume, Projects, LinkedIn

Automobile Engineering

  • Semester 1-3: Vehicle Mechanics, IC Engines, Chassis Design
  • Semester 4-5: AutoCAD, SolidWorks, Electric Vehicle Concepts
  • Semester 6: Internship at Auto Workshop or Plant
  • After Diploma:
    • D2D BTech Automobile or Join Automotive OEM
    • Focus on EV, Hybrid Vehicle Tech
    • Certification in AUTOSAR, MATLAB, CATIA

Mining Engineering

  • Semester 1-3: Mining Practices, Surveying, Rock Mechanics
  • Semester 4-5: Mine Planning, Blasting, Safety Regulations
  • Semester 6: Field Internship + Project (Mining Plan)
  • After Diploma:
    • D2D BTech Mining or Govt PSU Jobs (Coal India, SCCL)
    • Certifications in Mine Safety, DGMS
    • Higher studies or Management courses in Mining Mgmt

Chemical Engineering

  • Semester 1-3: Chemical Process, Thermodynamics, Material Science
  • Semester 4-5: Reactor Design, Safety, Plant Design
  • Semester 6: Project + Industry Training in Refineries/Plants
  • After Diploma:
    • D2D BTech Chemical or Join Pharma/Fertilizer Industry
    • Certifications: Process Safety, HAZOP
    • Govt Exams: ONGC, IOCL, HPCL, GATE

Metallurgical Engineering

  • Semester 1-3: Material Science, Physical Metallurgy
  • Semester 4-5: Heat Treatment, Welding, Foundry Tech
  • Semester 6: Plant Visit + Project Report
  • After Diploma:
    • D2D BTech Metallurgy or Join Steel/Foundry Industries
    • Advanced Courses in NDT, Welding Inspector
    • Govt Jobs: SAIL, BHEL, ISRO, BARC
